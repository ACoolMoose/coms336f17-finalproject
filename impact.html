Keyboard controls:

  • Camera controls:
  • w, a, s, d - move forward, left, back, right
  • r, f - move up, down
  • i, j, k, l - look up, left, down, right
  • I, J, K, L - orbit down, right, up, left
  • O - face origin
  • p - put camera at origin
  • W - decrease fov
  • S - increase fov
  • Impact Controls:
  • z, Z - decrease/increase impact force
  • x, X - decrease/increase material strength
  • c, C - decrease/increase crack range
